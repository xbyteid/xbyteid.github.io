Who I Am

Indonesian security researcher and builder. I've been in the trenches since 2024 — hunting bugs, breaking smart contracts, farming airdrops, and automating everything I can get my hands on.

My focus sits at the intersection of security research and Web3 infrastructure. I find vulnerabilities in web applications and decentralized protocols, audit Solidity smart contracts, and build AI-powered agents that automate repetitive workflows.
